ABOUT US

Delkar Consultancy and Agency is a dynamic and forward-thinking business founded in 2021, dedicated to providing expert services in business management, marketing, branding, and software development. We are a registered business with a team of seasoned professionals, including experienced marketers, business managers, and software engineers, each possessing over half a decade of experience in their respective fields. Our diverse backgrounds, coupled with comprehensive interpersonal and organizational skills, enable us to deliver high-quality services and products to our valued clients.
